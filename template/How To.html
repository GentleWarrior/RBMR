How Stem Cells are Collected;

Bone Marrow Donation

This is done by transferring stem cells from one person to another.

Umbilical Cord

Cord blood is the blood left in the placenta and umbilical cord after a baby is born.

Peripheral blood stem cells

A donor is given a daily infection that causes the bone marrow to release a lot of stem cells.
